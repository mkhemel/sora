Energy

Driving Bangladesh Towards Sustainable Energy Solutions

We are committed to leading Bangladesh towards a sustainable future through our innovative ventures in the energy sector.

The Bangladeshi government has an initial plan to produce 4100 MW of electricity from renewable sources, which, in the present situation, is only up to around 600–700 MW. With an intention to reduce dependency on fossil fuel electricity generation Govt. has initiated the plan to set grid tied solar based power generation projects in the Govt. owned nonagricultural lands. Power generated from the solar park will be directly feed into the grid on commercial basis.

Moreover, government has extended fiscal incentives including duty exemption on certain renewable energy products, e.g. solar panel, solar panel manufacturing accessories, Charge Controller etc.

At Sora Holdings, we believe in the power of sustainable energy to drive progress and prosperity.
